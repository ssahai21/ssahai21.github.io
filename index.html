Our Mission

To help foster respect and understanding within the global community by empowering refugees to share their stories and passions.

To give a voice to refugees and lessen xenophobia within communities.

To create a sense of respect for the passions and experience that refugees bring to the community by empowering them to share their stories.

To help foster respect and understanding in the global community through the arts.

Core Values

Respect for people's privacy and experiences. We always give our voices the option of anonymity and maintain an open mind during interviews.

Empathy for anyone and everyone. We keep this value in mind while working with all immigrants and hope to foster a similar sense of understanding within the communities that hear our stories.

Integrity in all our actions. While we do modify recordings to ensure our voices maintain privacy, we strive to provide the most accurate representation of every voice.
